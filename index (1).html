A clean, distraction-free Pomodoro timer to help you study and work more effectively — one session at a time.

Features

Everything you need
to stay on track

Focus sessions

25-minute deep work intervals to keep you in the zone and away from distractions.

Smart breaks

Short 5-min and long 15-min breaks to recharge your brain between sessions.

Task tracker

Add your tasks and tick them off as you power through each focus session.

Session stats

Track completed sessions, focus minutes, and breaks taken throughout your day.

How it works

Simple 4-step process

1

Add your tasks for the session

2

Start the 25-min focus timer

3

Work until the timer rings

4

Take a break, then repeat!
